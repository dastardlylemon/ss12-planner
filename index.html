WHO WE ARE

Habitask was built with love by Calvin Chan, James Wu, and Aman Agarwal in Los Angeles, California. This was a project for the 2013 SS12 Hackathon and is the product of 23 hours of caffeine and sadness. Habitask makes heavy use of the Google Calendar API.

Close

WHY HABITASK?

Students with ADHD often have a more difficult time with the “big picture” aspect of school projects. Habitask aims to help these students by letting teachers break up projects into smaller Milestones and Tasks, allowing the student to focus on a few immediate deadlines rather than a vague, amorphous due date.
